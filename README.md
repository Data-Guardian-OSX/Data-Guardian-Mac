## 🖌 Overview  

Data Guardian Professional Suite provides a fortified environment for storing and managing sensitive personal data. Every record — from passwords and card numbers to private notes and license keys — is protected by military-grade encryption.

The security model is local-first. Your encrypted database lives on your device, under your control, with no third-party access and no sync unless you choose it.

Organizing your data is straightforward. Use built-in templates for common record types or create custom layouts to match exactly what you need to store. Powerful search surfaces any record in an instant.

The app includes safeguards like auto-lock, clipboard clearing after paste, and breach-aware password generation to keep your security posture strong at every step.

Data Guardian Professional Suite is built for people who understand that good security is not optional — it is the foundation of a private digital life.
